Overview

Delivery Video Files and Live Video, from Mobile Platforms

KenCast's Vazzt Video-On-The-Move (VOTM) provides the video encoding, underlying network infrastructure, and playout capability to support video file delivery and live video capture from vehicles and aircraft on the move, and from users on foot to a central hub, and the ability to redistribute this live content from the central hub to users on the Internet or on private networks.

Find Enough Bandwidth for Quality Video & Deliver Flawlessly

When covering an event on the move, it is ususally difficult to find enough bandwidth for quality video, and the wireless bandwidth offered today, even when found, is often noisy. Vazzt VOTM creates enough bandwidth by

  1. accelerating delivery on any IP networks it finds
  2. bonding/aggregating the networks.

Vazzt then recovers from the damage caused by the noise by using the reknown KenCast Forward Error Correction (FEC) routines.

Keep It Simple - No Extra Hardware to Lug Around

Capturing video with a camera and a laptop or netbook while on the move is necessary for many people (New Gatherers, Police, Firemen, Emergency Workers, Soldiers, and many more). Almost all solutions offered today to support the efforts of users on the move require they add another piece of hardware, often larger/heavier than the camera and laptop. The Vazzt VOTM platform is ALL SOFTWARE - NO EXTRA BOX REQUIRED
